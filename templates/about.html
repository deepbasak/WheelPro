About OnyxForged

Masters of Premium Wheel Forging Since 2015

Our Story

OnyxForged was born from a passion for precision engineering and automotive excellence. Founded in 2015, we have established ourselves as a premier manufacturer of high-performance forged wheel rims, combining traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to create wheels that not only enhance performance but also embody the spirit of automotive artistry. Today, we serve discerning customers worldwide who demand nothing but the best for their luxury and sport vehicles.

Our Forging Excellence

Premium Materials

We source only the finest aerospace-grade aluminum alloys, ensuring exceptional strength-to-weight ratios and superior durability in every wheel we forge.

Precision Forging

Our state-of-the-art forging process uses pressures exceeding 8,000 tons, creating wheels with grain structures that are 30% stronger than cast alternatives.

Finishing Excellence

Each wheel undergoes meticulous finishing processes including CNC machining, custom powder coating, and rigorous quality control inspections.
